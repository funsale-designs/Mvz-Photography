My Story

I started my photography journey as a teenager with nothing but a borrowed camera and a dream. While others looked for perfection in sunsets or portraits, I found beauty in old buildings, rusty bikes, cracked pavements, and people just being themselves.

Photography taught me to *see*, not just look. To feel the colors in gray places and capture light in shadow. Every photo I take is a reminder that even the ordinary is extraordinary when you slow down and truly see it.
